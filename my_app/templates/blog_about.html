More on our processing method


Blood processing made easy and quick!

Artificial intelligence has made significant progress in closing the gap between human and computer capabilities, which is why we are using mathematical algorithms to process our blood samples.

The algorithm used for the blood testing is Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) which is a class of the deep learning, most generally employed for image classification. CNN is a supervised learning where it uses large, labelled datasets for training of the algorithm to predict output accurately. The blood droplet images will go through two processes which are feature extraction by the convolutional layer, and classification by the neural network layer before predicting an output.

Our algorithm has managed to reach a promising accuracy of 97%. Once we received your blood sample, and an image of the sample has been captured, the process of getting the results will be almost instantaneous.
